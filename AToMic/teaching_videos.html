教學影片區

教學影片區提供原子帝國軍玩家在學習業務開發、創業規劃心態、組織行銷、網路行銷等專業教學，讓玩家能在邊解任務過程邊學習提升自己能力。
此外，本區也是每名玩家的個人舞台，只要通過『勳章任務』中的『教育分享家』任務，就能在此區錄製個人主題式教學影片，
一般玩家可錄製三支影片（原子核則無數量限制），AToMic會幫每支影片作行銷宣傳，所有錄製影片的玩家也會獲得曝光度並建立自身在業界之專業可信度， 對於業務開發的成交轉換率及在組織團隊中的影響力、信任度、維持力將會有莫大的幫助，有目標企圖心的玩家務必善於運用。

The instructional videos section allows AToMic Imperial Force players to self learn business development,
entrepreneurial mindset planning, organizing marketing, network marketing and other professional classes, so that players can enhance their individual knowledge whilst completing a quest.

In addition, this section is also each player’s personal stage, when you have completed the “Education Quest" from “Medal quests" you can record a personal video tutorial to upload, each player may record up to 3 videos (Core members are not limited), AToMic will help with promoting your video and all players with videos will have exposure, in turn establish their own professional credibility in the industry, business development and influence in the team, such as trust, force maintaining will also be positively influenced, ambitious players be sure to make good use of this area.
