新手任務

所有剛登入遊戲的玩家第一步便是要先執行新手任務，任務完成的玩家才能報名新兵訓練營，領取遊戲主程式及武器裝備，
這是至關重要的，請玩家務必按照以下任務流程確實執行，任務過程中遇到的所有困難請向各位的引路人尋求支援。
First step after you login is to start on the novice tasks, only the player who have completed these tasks may register for Newbie’s training camp, receive game programs, weapons and equipments. What comes next is important, please make sure to follow the order the tasks are given in and any difficulties encountered in the process, you may seek support from your pathfinder.

STEP.1

下載新手任務啟動單，共兩張，並將其列印下來。(已由引路人取得者則略過此步驟)
Download the forms for Newbie Launch, should be a total of 2 pages, and print them out.
(If the forms have already been provided by your pathfinder then you may skip this step)

中文版 第一張下載點 第二張下載點

ENGLISH Page1 Download Page2 Download


STEP.2

依照下方教學影片完成新人啟動單，請認真看完並確實填寫，此步驟須在登入遊戲後的４８小時內完成。
Complete your Newbie Launch Form with the help of the video tutorials below, please read carefully and fill in properly, this step must be completed within 48 hours after you login to the game.

STEP.3

觀看以下由美國IMD所傳授的開始之前三步驟影片，並把重點記錄下來。（須看三遍以上）
Watch the following video by an IMD from the United States he teaches three-step set up, focus and take notes.
(Must watch more than 3 times)

STEP.4

完成以上三步驟後，立即向你的引路人報名新兵訓練營，把握時間，你的競爭對手們可不會等你。
After completing the above three steps immediately register for Newbie’s training camp with your pathfinder, seize your time for your competitors will not wait for you.

STEP.5

相信自己，下定決心開始行動！將邀約狀況隨時回報給你的引路人，帶上啟動單，迎接新兵訓練。
勳章任務是原子帝國軍遊戲主軸，必須成為原子帝國軍(通過新手任務及新兵訓練的玩家)才能承接，破解任務將獲得經驗值及勳章。
Believe in yourself, find your purpose and take action! Report back to your pathfinder after every invitation, always carry your Launch Form, and get ready for your Training Camp. The Quests for Medals are the bases of AToMic Imperial Force, and you must be an AToMic Imperial Soldier (through completion of Newbie training and AToMic boot camp) to be allowed to undertake all tasks, crack the tasks to gain experience and medals.

通過新兵訓練的戰士，才是原子帝國軍！
