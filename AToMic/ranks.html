帝國軍階

原子帝國軍階有別於一般國家政府單位軍隊的階級制度，是以其轄下戰士的直屬精銳數量判定，綜合戰力經驗值則影響排行名次。
直屬精銳數量增加減少將直接影響到軍階高低而有所升降，而經驗值則概括了獎勵成就、勳章任務及特殊競賽加總而定。

Unlike ordinary government rankings, AToMic Imperial Force judges by the number of elite players directly in your team,
and comprehensive combat capability experience affects your ranking.
Increase and decrease in the number of your elite players will also directly impact your rank level, and reward achievements,
medals and special task contest completion will in turn become your experience.

戰士排行

戰士排行榜是原子帝國全體戰士追求的目標與肯定，排行前十者為原子帝國”十戰將”，而帝國戰神更是傳奇般的至高無上殊榮， 除了能獲得眾人尊崇及取之不盡用之不竭的財富外，更能無條件直接進入原子帝國名人堂，在帝國紀元上名留青史。
此外在每月的原子總動員盛會上，當天排行第一名的戰士可直接獲得現金一千元獎勵及下個月原子帝國主城的免費使用權!

AToMics’ Players Ranking is every single soldier’s goal and desired recognition, ranking top ten of AToMic Imperial Force "The 10 Master Generals” and “Empire Warlord” is of supreme honor, in addition to earning respect and inexhaustible wealth, he/she can directly and unconditionally enter the AToMic Empire Hall of Fame, and go down in history in the Empire era.
On the day of AToMic general mobilization occurring once every month, the soldier first in rank will receive NT1,000 in cash bonus and free access to the main fortress of the AToMic Imperial Force!
